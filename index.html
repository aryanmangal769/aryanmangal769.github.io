Carnegie Mellon University - Pypose Project

(Dec 2021 - May 2022, Pittsburgh, USA)

  • Supervisor: Prof. Sebastian Scherer (Personal Webpage)

Griffin - Unmanned Ground Vehicle

(August 2019 - August 2021, DTU, New Delhi, India)

Developed an autonomous ground vehicle to represent Delhi Technological University in the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition at Oakland University, Rochester, Michigan.

  • Engineered the navigation stack of a self-driving rover using Robot Operating System (ROS) with lane detection and obstacle avoidance.
  • Utilized ROS packages such as Slam\_gmapping, Move\_base, and Costmap for synchronization with real-world environments.
  • Implemented sensor fusion with IMU, GPS, Lidar, odometers, micro-controllers, and actuators.
  • Supervisor: Dr. S. Indu (Personal Webpage)
